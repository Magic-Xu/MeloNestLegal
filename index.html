MeloNest

A lightweight local Android music app for generating, importing, keeping, and replaying your own private music library.

What MeloNest Does

Generate musicCreate songs from a prompt through a configured generation gateway.
Import locallyImport audio, or import a video and keep only its audio track in app-private storage.
Play privatelyReplay generated and imported tracks without feeds, accounts, or popups.
